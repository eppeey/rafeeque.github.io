I was born in Kerala, India, into a middle-class family. Throughout my academic journey, I was an average student, often just passing my courses. During my school days, my dream was to become a police officer, but as I grew older, I became increasingly fascinated with technology. I pursued my undergraduate degree (BCA) and later my postgraduate degree (MCA) at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham.

My journey in the tech world has been a continuous learning process, and I am still eager to expand my knowledge and skills.

This journey has been a transformational one, taking me from my childhood dream of becoming a police officer to embracing the ever-evolving world of technology.
